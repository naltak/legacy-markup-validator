How to Provide Feedback on W3C's Validation Service

If your page doesn't validate, and you don't know why, or
you have a question about HTML or validation

First, check the Web authoring frequently asked questions (FAQs) to see if your question has been answered there.

If it hasn't, you can send it to one of the following forums:

  • Usenet newsgroup: comp.infosystems.www.authoring.html
  • HTML Writers Guild mailing lists

Each of these forums have plenty of experienced HTML authors who are willing to share their expertise. (If you are commenting on a specific page, be sure to provide a URL when you ask your question!)

If you are interested in helping to improve this service, by writing code or just providing ideas,

Please join and contribute to the www-validator mailing list.

If you just have a small patch and don't want to join the list, feel free to send it directly to the list.

If none of the above options are appropriate,

You can send me e-mail, and I will try to respond as soon as I can. I'm sorry that I may not be able to reply to every message I receive.

Please try to use one of the online forums mentioned above; much of the time others will be able to help, and having the discussion in an open publicly-archived forum has many other benefits as well.
